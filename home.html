Development
The purpose of this project was to gain more experience programming in React by creating a list interface. For this assignment, I created a simple shopping website that displays a list of items for purchase as well as a shopping cart that sums up the total price of the purchase.
Introduction: The Application
The application is a shopping website for fresh produce. The goal of this website is to allow users to look at produce and to add items to their shopping cart for checkout. In the shopping cart section, which is located at the bottom of the page, the users also have the option to add more items or remove items from their cart. Furthermore, they are able to see the total price of their pruchase, and the price is adjusted as users modify their purchase. The value of this shopping website is that it is easy to navigate and use. This is because users are able to look at the products and view their shopping cart all in one page.
Usability Principles
  • Affordance: I wanted users to be able to navigate through the website without actually having to think about how the website should be used. To make it easy for the users to use the website, I clearly placed the filter buttons and the sort by buttons near the products, and I made sure to include two different filter boxes for each filter category (season and size).
  • Consistency: Each product item is displayed consistently. It includes information regarding its price, season, and size. Each product "card" also includes an "Add to Cart" button, which allows users to easily add products to their cart. In the shopping cart section, the product "card" changes the "Add to Cart" button with a button that allows users to decremement the quantity of the item, view the quantity of the item in the cart, and increase the quantity of the item. It also includes a separate "Remove" button, which removes the product altogether from the shopping cart regardless of the original quantity.
  • Visibility: Users are able to view everything, from the products to the shopping cart, all in one page. I also only included one dropdown menu to minimize hidden information on the page.
